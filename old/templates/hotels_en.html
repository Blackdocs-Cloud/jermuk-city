Armenia Hotel and SPA

Armenia Hotel and SPA features a garden, terrace, a restaurant and bar in Jermuk. This 5-star hotel offers room service, a 24-hour front desk and free WiFi. The hotel has an indoor pool, sauna, evening entertainment and a shared lounge.

All rooms come with a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, fridge, an electric tea pot, a shower, free toiletries and a desk. The rooms include a private bathroom with a hairdryer, while selected rooms also feature a balcony and others also feature city views. Guest rooms have a safety deposit box. A continental breakfast is available each morning at the hotel.

Armenia Hotel and SPA offers 5-star accommodations with a spa center and playground. You can play pool, ping pong and darts at this 5-star hotel, and the area is popular for skiing and cycling.

The nearest airport is Zvartnots International, 106 miles from the accommodation, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
